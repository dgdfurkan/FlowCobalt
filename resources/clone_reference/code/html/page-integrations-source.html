FAQs

Can’t find the answer here? Contact our support team.

What are integrations in Relevance AI?

Integrations connect your agents to external tools—so they can take action in apps like Gmail, Slack, Notion, and HubSpot.

What’s the difference between an integration, a trigger, and a tool step?

An integration connects a platform to your agent. A trigger starts a workflow when something happens (like a new email). A tool step is what your agent uses to take action inside that platform—like sending a message or updating a CRM.

How many integrations can I connect?

As many as you need. There’s no hard limit—connect every tool your workflow depends on.

Can I link multiple accounts from the same service?

Yes. Ideal for teams or agencies managing multiple brands or clients.

What if the integration I need isn’t available?

Use a webhook or custom API tool step to connect almost any service—no need to wait for a native integration.
